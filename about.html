Dr. Kristofor Kalvig

About:

Kristofor J. Kalvig, DPM, AACFAS, is a foot and ankle surgical podiatric specialist.

As a surgical podiatrist Dr. Kalvig has specialized training to deal with all aspects of the foot and ankle.

Podiatry encompasses both pediatric and adult foot and ankle medicine. Podiatry is a specialty service that utilizes evidence-based data in the treatment of simple and complex lower extremity pathology.

Education:

Medical School: Temple University School of Podiatry

Residency: William S. Middleton VA Hospital with additional training at Watertown, University of Wisconsin, and Marshfield Orthopedics
